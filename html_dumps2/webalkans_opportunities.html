About WeBalkans.eu

The WeBalkans.eu portal is a one-stop shop for news, stories, funding opportunities, and related information on cooperation between the European Union and the Western Balkans. It is part of the EU Regional Communication Programme for the Western Balkans, managed by the European Commission and implemented by an external contracted agency, WEGlobal SRL.

The programme seeks to raise awareness and increase knowledge and understanding of EU-Western Balkan relations and of EU enlargement policy.
